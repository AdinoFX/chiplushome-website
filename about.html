Meet Our Founder

Chinenye Eunice Okeke

Representing Nigerian homeowners and future homeowners, Chinenye is committed to listening to her clients' needs and utilizing her keen negotiating skills to ensure a successful transaction. Raised in the South West Region of Nigeria, Chinenye understands the numerous intangible benefits of life in the region, with its unmatched weather, culture, and diversity.

After graduating from Ogun State University with a bachelor's degree in Business Administration, Chinenye joined the real estate profession. Over the past 5 years, she has become an esteemed realtor, highly regarded by her clients and professionals in the field. She is well-connected, actively involved in the community, and utilizes a team of professional service providers to assist with all her clients' buying and selling needs.
